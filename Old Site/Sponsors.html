The Cytometry for Life program is an extension of the Purdue University Cytometry Laboratories within the Bindley Bioscience Center at Purdue University's Discovery Park. The program is entirely driven to change the lives of people in resource-poor countries, particularly Africa where AIDS has devastated country after country. It is our contention that unless we make really practical projects, and do things that actually impact these countries today, we will not be effective.

This program is currently open for sponsorship.

Purdue University is contributing significant resources to this project.

 

Current Major Sponsors:

  • Purdue University Cytometry Laboratories
  • Icyt Visionary Bioscience
  • Parker-Life Sciences
